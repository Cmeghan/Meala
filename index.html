About Meala

Meala is a new salon in London, owned and operated by multi-award winning stylist Andrew Thomas Jones.ATJ has been working in the industy for over 15 years, and has worked with some of the top brands in the world.They are currently a brand ambassador for Redken. Meala is currently based in Chelsea, however, on Tuesdays and Wednesdays, we offer a mobile service directly to your home, provided you live within Central London. Feel free to contact us below should you wish to book a consultation.
